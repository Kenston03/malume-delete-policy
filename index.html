Delete Your Malume Account

This page explains how users can delete their account and associated data from the Malume app.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the Malume app
  2. Go to the Profile page
  3. Select "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

Alternatively, you can email us at support@malume.app with your request.

Data Deleted

  • Your account information (name, email, phone)
  • Your alert or chat history

Data Retained

We do not retain any personal data after deletion unless legally required.

For more information, contact us at support@malume.app.
